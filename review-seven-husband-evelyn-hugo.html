The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo

Taylor Jenkins Reid

The rating is...

What I thought...

The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo" by Taylor Jenkins Reid is a captivating and intricately woven story that takes readers on an emotional journey through the life of the enigmatic Hollywood icon, Evelyn Hugo. The book is a masterpiece, skillfully blending elements of glamour, love, ambition, and sacrifice.

Reid's storytelling prowess shines as she narrates Evelyn's life, detailing her rise to fame, her tumultuous relationships, and the secrets she has carefully guarded. The narrative style, with its alternating timelines and perspectives, keeps the reader engaged and eager to uncover the layers of Evelyn's complex persona.

What truly sets this book apart is its characters. Evelyn Hugo is a magnetic and flawed protagonist whose choices and vulnerabilities resonate deeply. Her relationships, especially with her seven husbands, are portrayed with such depth and authenticity that each marriage feels distinct and pivotal in Evelyn's evolution.

The exploration of themes like identity, love, and the price of fame is profound and thought-provoking. Reid skillfully delves into the challenges faced by women in the entertainment industry, shedding light on the sacrifices made to navigate a male-dominated world.

The writing is evocative and immersive, transporting readers to the glitz and glamour of old Hollywood while also delving into the more poignant and intimate moments of Evelyn's life. The pacing is excellent, maintaining a perfect balance between revealing revelations and maintaining suspense.

Overall, "The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo" is a mesmerizing tale that lingers in the mind long after the last page. Taylor Jenkins Reid's ability to craft a compelling narrative filled with rich characters and profound emotions makes this book an absolute must-read. It's a story that resonates on multiple levels, leaving an indelible mark on the reader's heart.
